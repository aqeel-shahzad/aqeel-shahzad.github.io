Airline Customer Recommendation Analytics
01

Airline Customer Satisfaction Analytics

Analysed airline review data to identify service themes, sentiment patterns, and value-for-money signals associated with customer recommendation behaviour.

Python NLP Machine Learning Sentiment Analysis
Business problem: What drives customer recommendation and dissatisfaction in airline reviews?
Methods: Text analytics, sentiment analysis, classification modelling, TF-IDF, VADER, RoBERTa, and feature importance.

Hospital Capacity Planning
03

Hospital Capacity Planning and Optimisation

Built a mixed-integer linear programming model to optimise weekly procedure-room configurations for a hospital GI unit over a 26-week planning horizon.

R MILP Optimisation Healthcare Analytics
Business problem: How should a hospital configure procedure rooms each week to meet diagnostic and therapeutic demand at minimum cost?
Methods: Mixed-integer linear programming, capacity constraints, setup-cost modelling, clinician availability constraints, and solver validation.
Outcome: Produced a 26-week room configuration plan with a minimum total cost of 2,265,600, using an average of seven rooms per week while meeting all demand.

Current dissertation project

Selected to work with MindWise on decision-focused analytics for service prioritisation.

Currently one of five students selected to work with MindWise on a dissertation project exploring how analytics can support fairer and more effective service allocation when resources are limited.

The project builds on a decision-focused analytics approach: instead of judging models only by prediction accuracy, it evaluates whether model outputs lead to better allocation decisions. The workflow links prediction models with knapsack optimisation, decision regret analysis, and fairness-aware constraints to understand which service requests should be prioritised under staff or time capacity limits.

This is especially meaningful because MindWise is a leading Northern Ireland mental health charity and a founding member of Mental Health UK, which brings together four national mental health charities working across the UK. MindWise provides over 30 services across Northern Ireland, supporting people affected by mental health issues, carers and families.
