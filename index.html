Airline Customer Recommendation Analytics
01

Airline Customer Satisfaction Analytics

Analysed airline review data to identify service themes, sentiment patterns, and value-for-money signals associated with customer recommendation behaviour.

Python NLP Machine Learning Sentiment Analysis
Business problem: What drives customer recommendation and dissatisfaction in airline reviews?
Methods: Text analytics, sentiment analysis, classification modelling, TF-IDF, VADER, RoBERTa, and feature importance.
Outcome: Built models to predict airline recommendation behaviour from service ratings and review text, with the best model achieving 96.3% accuracy and 98.8% ROC-AUC.
